ABOUT ME!

Comfort Museo

Comfort Museo is the Founder and CEO of Qigg, an e-commerce platform for B2B Services. Comfort is passionate about Entrepreneurship, having worked actively with SMEs on a business development role for 2+ years. Up untill June 30th, Comfort was part of a 6-month Incubation Program by Techbridge Invest Africa. Comfort has gained extensive knowledge on how to grow a Start-up from a business perspective, and she would like to now improve her Tech capabilities in order to be a well-rounded leader. For this reason Comfort has joined Moringa School, one of the best programming bootcamps in Kenya, and Africa at large. She hopes that this dive into a some-what challenging experience will be a value addition as she builds one of the best tech company of our time.
